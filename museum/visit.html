Accessibility

The museum is fully accessible to visitors with disabilities. Wheelchairs are available at the front desk. We also offer guided tours for visually and hearing-impaired visitors. For more information, please contact us directly.

Visitor Guidelines

To ensure an enjoyable experience for all, we ask that visitors adhere to the following guidelines:

    No flash photography in the exhibits.

    Food and drinks are not permitted inside the galleries.

    Children must be supervised by an adult at all times.

    Please refrain from touching the exhibits unless indicated.
